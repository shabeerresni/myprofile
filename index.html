From Strategy to Stack — Building Rayenna Energy's Complete Digital & Technology Ecosystem

As Honorary Independent Director at Rayenna Energy, I led the company's early-stage foundation across business planning, sales organisation design, HR frameworks, and product positioning — while simultaneously architecting and delivering its entire technology ecosystem from the ground up.

The centrepiece is an enterprise-grade internal business platform — a purpose-built operating system for a solar EPC company — featuring a fully customised CRM covering the end-to-end customer lifecycle, an automated proposal engine, and an AI-powered Analytics Command Centre delivering real-time business intelligence across sales, operations, and finance.

On the customer-facing side, I conceived and delivered rayennaenergy.com — a conversion-optimised, mobile-first corporate website built on Astro — complete with an interactive Solar Calculator and a content-rich knowledge hub.

Crowning the digital stack is Ray — Rayenna's always-on AI sales advisor, built on Claude. Ray engages prospects in real time in both English and Malayalam, guides them through their solar journey, and routes qualified leads directly to the sales team — closing the loop between digital presence and revenue conversion.
